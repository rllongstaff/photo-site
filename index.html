Using multiple top of the line cameras with dual memory card slots and carrying an array of lenses, you can relax and enjoy your wedding photography experience knowing that you're in safe hands. ENGAGEMENT PHOTOGRAPHY

Engagement Sessions

Engagement sessions are an integral part of your wedding photography and are an added feature of some of our wedding packages. They provide diversity within your imagery, where the clothing, location and even the season can be different to that of your wedding day. They are also a great way for the two of you to get used to working with your photographer and become more comfortable in front of the camera, resulting in better wedding pictures on your big day. We even offer a bespoke guestbook for the day of your wedding, filled with breathtaking photographs from your engagement session, giving you a more personal keepsake that goes far beyond an off-the-shelf guestbook. If you're engaged and looking for a wedding photographer in Buckinghamshire or anywhere in the U.K. get in touch today to find out what we can do for your wedding and engagement pictures.
