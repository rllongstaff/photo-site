Buckinghamshire Based Wedding Photographer, Gregory Cusick

Gregory Cusick specialises in weddings, family and portrait photography. Primarily covering Bucks, Berks and Herts but offers services to the whole of the U.K.

More than a photographer.

His grandfather was an artist and a huge influence on Greg, teaching him to draw and paint from an early age. This influence is clearly seen in his work as a photographer, balancing light, shadows, composition, emotion and technical knowhow to create the breathtaking images he has come to be known for.

Belief

It is our aim to consistently produce the highest standard of photographs, albums, prints and products for our clients.

Assurance

When you book with us, you will be working directly with Greg every step of the way. With a friendly and unobtrusive approach, plus a wealth of experience, you can relax and enjoy every moment, confident that you are in the best hands.
